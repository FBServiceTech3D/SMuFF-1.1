M203 [ Xn [ Dn ] | Yn [ Dn ] | Zn [ Dn Fn ] ]
---------------------------------------------
Xn 		the maximum speed for the X axis (Selector)
Yn 		the maximum speed for the Y axis (Revolver)
Zn 		the maximum speed for the Z axis (Feeder)
Dn 		the delay between pulses in microseconds
Fn 		the insert speed for the Z axis (Feeder)
