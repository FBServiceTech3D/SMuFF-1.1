M503 [ Sn [W]]
-----------
Sn 		the category to show, i.e.: 
            1 = Basic settings
            2 = Stepper settings
            3 = TMC settings
            4 = Servo settings
            5 = Material settings
            6 = Tool swaps
            7 = Servo mappings (V6S only)
            8 = Feed states (Splitter only)
W 		create output for WebInterface

Without S parameter all data will be shown (does not apply for WebInterface)
