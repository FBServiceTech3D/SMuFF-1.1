M914 Xn | Yn | Zn [ Cn | Tn ]
-----------------------------
Xn 		the stall sensitivity for the Selector (n: 0..255)
Yn 		the stall sensitivity for the Revolver (n: 0..255)
Zn 		the stall sensitivity for the Feeder (n: 0..255)
Cn 		the cool step value (n: 0..31)
Tn 		the stall trigger value (n: 0..255)
