M111 Sn
------------
Sn		Change debug level to n (binary encoded): 
		  0 = NONE

		  1 = DEBUG
		  2 = WARNING
		  4 = INFO
		  8 = SPECIAL
		 16 = DEVELOPMENT (1)
		 32 = DEVELOPMENT (2)
		 64 = DEVELOPMENT (3)

		Examples:
		  6 = WARNING & INFO (default)
		  7 = DEBUG & WARNING & INFO
		 15 = DEBUG & WARNING & INFO & SPECIAL
		 31 = DEBUG & WARNING & INFO & SPECIAL & DEVELOPMENT (1)
